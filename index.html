Pokemon Sun and Moon

Aloha new adventurers

Welcome to the Alola region, home of four different islands; a sleeping guardian resides on each island! You'll be guided by Professor Kukui, an exuberant and amiable young graduate of the academy. Professor Kukui will provide you with a partner to accompany you on your island trials and the rotom pokédex; play with the pokédex to get a better perspective of each island's native pokémons.

Here's what to expect:

  • 81 native pokemons, not including the exclusive Alolan forms of old, familiar faces!
  • New Legendaries
  • Unlocking the power of Z crystals to cause cataclysmic damage
  • A brand new feature called poké ride that allows you to call on certain pokémons to aid you in the fields
  • The Aether Foundation
  • A group of mysterious creatures that threatens the existence of the world

IT'S YOUR BOI GUZMA

Renovate an island and reap the rewards

x

Isle Abeens

The first island you'll explore. You can collect poke beans from the giant beanstalk; using the beans, you can upgrade your islands and affect the activities on each island.

x

Isle Aplenny

Gather berries from across the Alola region and plant them here. Don't forget to water your garden while you watch your garden flourish!

x

Isle Aphun

Send your pokemon into the darkest caverns to discover treasures such as the coveted gold bottle cap and evolution stones.

x

Isle Evelup

Enjoy the dojo with your pokemons. There are a variety of energy drinks that will help them grow stronger in a preferred stat. Depending on the level of the dojo, a pokémon can gain up to 4 ev every half hour session.

x

Isle Avue

Relax at the hotsprings with your pokemons; spending time in the hotsprings will help raise the happiness, but wait, don't forget about them for over 24 hours or they'll start to lose happiness. As a bonus, you can also incubate eggs within the hotsprings to help hatch them.
