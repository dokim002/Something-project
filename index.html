Pokemon Sun and Moon

Aloha new adventurers

Welcome to the Alola region, home of four different islands, each watched over by a sleeping guardian spoken of by the elders! You'll be guided by Professor Kukui, an exuberant and amiable young graduate of the academy. Professor Kukui will provide you with a partner to accompany you on your island trials and a rotom pokédex; a helpful guide that you can interact with.

Here's what to expect:

  • 81 native pokemons, not including the exclusive Alolan forms of old, familiar faces!
  • New Legendaries
  • Unlocking the power of Z crystals to cause cataclysmic damage
  • A brand new feature called poké ride that allows you to call on certain pokémons to aid you in the fields
  • The Aether Foundation, a group of people that dedicates their lives to helping those in need
  • A group of mysterious creatures that threatens the existence of the world

Renovate an island and reap the rewards

Isle Abeens

The first island you'll explore. You can collect poke beans from the giant beanstalk that you can use to upgrade your islands and effect the activities on each island.

x

Isle Aplenny

Gather berries from the Alola region and plant them here. Wait for 72 hours and watch your garden flourish!

x

Isle Aphun

Send your pokemon into the darkest caverns to discover treasures such as the coveted gold bottle cap and evolution stones.

x

Isle Evelup

Enjoy the dojo with your pokemons, giving them a variety of energy drinks that will help them grow stronger.

x

Isle Avue

Relax at the hotsprings with your pokemons; spending time in the hotsprings will help raise the happiness, but wait, don't forget about them for over 24 hours or they'll start to lose happiness. As a bonus, you can also incubate eggs within the hotsprings to help hatch them.
